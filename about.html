About Watts ATP Contractor

ATP Approved Contractor - Nebraska Licensed #54690-25

Northeast Nebraska's Trusted ADA Accessibility Specialist

Your ADA Accessibility Expert

Watts ATP Contractor is Norfolk, Nebraska's premier ADA accessibility and safety modification specialist. With Nebraska contractor license #54690-25 and ATP (Assistive Technology Professional) approval, we deliver expert solutions that help individuals live safely and independently in their homes.

We specialize in wheelchair ramp installation, grab bar placement, bathroom accessibility modifications, and non-slip flooring solutions. Every project is designed to meet or exceed ADA guidelines while providing maximum comfort and safety for our clients.

Our focus on accessibility sets us apart. We understand the unique challenges faced by seniors, individuals with disabilities, and families seeking safer living environments. We bring both technical expertise and compassionate service to every project.

Our Mission

To make every home in Nebraska safer and more accessible. Watts ATP Contractor combines professional craftsmanship with ADA expertise to create environments that empower independence, dignity, and peace of mind for every client we serve.

We believe everyone deserves to feel safe and comfortable in their own home, regardless of mobility challenges or age-related limitations.
