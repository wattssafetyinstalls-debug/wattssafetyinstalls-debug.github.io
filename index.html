Specialized Services for Enhanced Home Safety and Accessibility

Our core specialization in accessibility and safety modifications includes comprehensive bathroom remodeling for seniors and individuals with mobility challenges. We install zero-step showers with proper grading to prevent water accumulation, commercial-grade grab bars rated for 500+ pounds, and non-slip flooring options that reduce fall risks while maintaining beautiful aesthetics. For homeowners concerned about senior safety, we offer complete home assessments and modifications including widened doorways, lowered countertops, improved lighting, and emergency alert system installations. We understand that creating a safe home environment involves both structural modifications and practical safety features that work together to prevent accidents and enhance independence.
