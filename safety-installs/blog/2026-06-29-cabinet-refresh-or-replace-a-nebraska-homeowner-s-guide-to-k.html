The Case for Cabinet Refinishing: A Fresh Look for Less

Refinishing your cabinets essentially means giving your existing cabinet boxes and doors a cosmetic facelift. This typically involves thorough cleaning, sanding down the old finish, making minor repairs, and then applying new paint or stain. It's an excellent option if your current cabinet boxes are structurally sound, well-built, and you're happy with the existing layout of your kitchen.

Pros of Refinishing:

* Cost-Effective: This is often the biggest driver. Refinishing can be significantly cheaper than replacing, saving you anywhere from 50% to 70% of the cost of new cabinets. * Less Disruptive: While still a project, refinishing generally takes less time than a full replacement.
