Winter Home Prep Checklist: Nebraska Edition

By Justin Watts · Watts Safety Installs

Nebraska Winter Prep Starts Now!

Hey folks, Justin from Watts Safety Installs here. Let's get your Norfolk home ready before the snow and ice hit hard this season!

Protect Your Pipes!

Disconnect garden hoses and insulate outdoor faucets. Consider heat tape for vulnerable pipes. Prevent costly bursts when temperatures drop below freezing.

Seal Out Cold Air.

Check windows and doors for drafts. Apply weatherstripping or caulk to keep warm air in. Save energy and stay cozy all winter long!

Furnace Tune-Up Time!

Change your furnace filter and schedule a professional inspection. An efficient furnace is crucial for surviving harsh Nebraska winters safely and warmly.
