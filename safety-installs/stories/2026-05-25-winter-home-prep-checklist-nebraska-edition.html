Winter Home Prep Checklist: Nebraska Edition

By Justin Watts · Watts Safety Installs

Get Ready, Nebraska Winter!

Winter's coming! First, check your furnace filter. A clean filter means efficient heating and lower bills. Easy win!

Seal Up Drafts & Leaks

Those chilly Nebraska winds find every crack! Seal windows, doors, and any foundation gaps to keep the warmth inside your home.

Protect Your Pipes Now

Frozen pipes are a nightmare. Disconnect garden hoses and insulate exposed pipes, especially in unheated areas like garages. Don't risk it!

Carbon Monoxide Safety First

With heaters running, ensure your CO detectors work. It’s crucial for every Norfolk, NE home. Test them monthly for peace of mind!
