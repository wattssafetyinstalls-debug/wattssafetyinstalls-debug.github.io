Winter Home Prep Checklist: Nebraska Edition

By Justin Watts · Watts Safety Installs

Get Ready, Nebraska!

Winter's coming fast! Let's make sure your home is safe and cozy before the first big snow hits Norfolk.

Insulate Those Pipes!

Frozen pipes are a nightmare. Wrap outdoor spigots and exposed indoor pipes, especially in unheated areas.

Seal Out Drafts!

Check windows and doors for drafts. A little caulk or weatherstripping saves a lot on your heating bill.

CO & Smoke Alarms

Crucial for Nebraska winters! Test your detectors and replace batteries. Consider a smart CO detector.
