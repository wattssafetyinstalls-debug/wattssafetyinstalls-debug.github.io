ADA-Compliant Wheelchair Ramp Installation Services

Watts Safety Installs provides professional wheelchair ramp installation throughout Northeast Nebraska, serving a 65-mile radius around Norfolk. As your local accessibility specialists, we expertly install ADA-compliant wheelchair ramps in Norfolk, Battle Creek, Pierce, and throughout Madison County and Antelope County. Whether you need a residential wheelchair ramp for aging in place or commercial accessibility solutions, we offer free consultations, same-day service availability, and lifetime craftsmanship warranties. Trust our licensed Nebraska contractors for wheelchair ramp installations that meet ADA standards and enhance mobility independence.

home wheelchair ramp requirements Norfolk Nebraska

Every ramp we install meets or exceeds residential code: maximum 1:12 slope (1 inch of rise per 12 inches of run), minimum 36-inch clear width, handrails between 34–38 inches high when rise is over 6 inches, 5-foot landings at top and bottom, and non-slip walking surface. We handle the details so you can just roll safely and confidently.
