Aging in Place: Make Your Home Safe

By Justin Watts · Watts ATP Contractor

Age Comfortably, Stay Safe!

Your home can be your safest haven. Let's make smart updates now so you can enjoy independent living for years to come.

Prevent Costly Falls

Clear pathways, remove throw rugs, and ensure good lighting. Simple steps prevent serious trips and falls, keeping you safe at home.

Light Up Your Life!

Good lighting is crucial, especially on stairs and in hallways. Consider smart lights for convenience. It's a game-changer for homes across Nebraska!

Bathroom Safety First!

Install grab bars in showers and next to toilets. Non-slip mats are a must. These small changes make a big difference for daily safety.
