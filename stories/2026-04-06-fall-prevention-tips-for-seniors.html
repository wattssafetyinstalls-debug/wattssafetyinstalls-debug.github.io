Fall Prevention Tips for Seniors

By Justin Watts · Watts ATP Contractor

Stay Steady, Stay Safe!

Falls are a big concern, but many are preventable! Let's look at easy ways to keep you or your loved ones safe.

Clear the Clutter!

Trip hazards like rugs, cords, and clutter are major culprits. Keep walkways clear and secure loose carpets.

Strength & Balance Matters

Regular, gentle exercises improve strength and balance. Here in Norfolk, NE, we see how vital staying active is for independence.

Light Up Your Path

Good lighting prevents stumbles. Add nightlights, fix dim bulbs, and ensure stairwells are brightly lit day and night.
