Fall Prevention Tips for Seniors

By Justin Watts · Watts ATP Contractor

Keep Your Balance, Seniors!

Falls are preventable! As Justin from Watts ATP in Norfolk, NE, let's talk simple steps to keep you safe and independent at home.

Clear Paths, Brighter Homes

Remove tripping hazards like rugs and clutter. Good lighting, especially at night, helps you see clearly and avoid stumbles around the house.

Install Smart Safety Aids

Grab bars in bathrooms and sturdy handrails on stairs are game-changers. My team in Nebraska can help with professional installation for peace of mind.

Stay Active, Review Meds

Gentle exercises improve strength and balance. Regularly review medications with your doctor; some can cause dizziness or drowsiness.
