Fall Prevention Tips for Seniors

By Justin Watts · Watts ATP Contractor

Prevent Falls, Live Fuller!

Nobody wants a fall. Let's look at simple ways to keep you safe and confident in your home every day. Your well-being is our priority!

Clear Paths, Bright Lights

Trip hazards are common! Remove clutter, secure rugs, and ensure bright lighting. We help Norfolk, NE folks stay safe at home.

Stay Strong, Stay Balanced

Regular, gentle exercise improves balance and strength. Discuss medication side effects with your doctor, too; they can impact stability.

Smart Home Safety Upgrades

Install grab bars in bathrooms. Use handrails on stairs. Wear supportive, non-slip footwear. Small changes make a big difference!
