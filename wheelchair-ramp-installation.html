What makes a wheelchair ramp ADA compliant?

ADA compliant wheelchair ramps require proper slope ratio (1:12 for most applications), minimum width of 36 inches, level landings, handrails on ramps over 6 feet high, and non-slip surfaces. We ensure all ramps meet ADA guidelines and local building codes for safety and accessibility.
